Me & Books

2008 ~ current

Thoughts

I think books are always cool. I also think this is something that I will always agree upon (controversial at my school maybe, which is why I defended books against it during my ASPR interview...). There are just so many benefits to reading. The flow state is something that I greatly cherish. I love learning about different things through non-fiction (although I fall asleep more easily when reading non fiction > fiction). There are just so many worlds, characters out there that we will never get to experience if not for books.

Given this it wouldn't be surprising if I had to wear glasses as a kid for reading under the dark. I also have reached the platinum level of the book-buying app I use (I just can't seem to read on a screen!). My bookshelf is too full that I have to pile books horizontally on top and I pay extra fees are the airport because my bag is too heavy with books. I dont know, I just love books.

I usually read SF, dystopian short stories (korean), some classics and novels about people.

If you have any recommendations, feel free to reach out! By default, though, I am hesitant to provide recs because I think reading is done best if you enjoy the book you are reading. This applies the other way too, sometimes choose not to read recs because I might enjoy other books more. Still I'm always open to talking about em :)
