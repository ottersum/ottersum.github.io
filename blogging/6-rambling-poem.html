CHAPTER 2.6

rambling poem about mountains and stones

2025/4/18

I wrote this as a part of english class but i wanted to save it here

i quietly stared at the tiny mountain being surprised to see myself sitting here like a stone

people came and eventually went

the sun rose and fell

birds hooted then slept

winds gushed and faded

the mountain and stone developed a dent

at some point time became hard to tell

maybe someone wept

the face reflected on the puddle was ragged

the tall tree had a shadow that lengthed shortened and naturally disappeared like the stone to but the wind imbued a sense of unwelcome freshness in it that the sky above it looked bluer than it should be as if it were the sea falling to swallow everything on earth

remembering mountains words that maybe humans are just visitors on earth was a struggle because all that came to mind was the way his eyes sparkled when sat across the dinner table

sitting here for a while the plants grew and danced along the wind and its mellifluous melody and turbulent tempo and the bugs chirped while gleaming its shell as if they were the stars on the dark night sky and wishing that he was there looking down at her

there is a grey stone reflected on the sky that is unable to move and sadly stuck in the ground or more like attached to a mountain because it shared so much of its mineral composition

the rain erodes parts that the stone shared with the mountain and the green weed and purple flowers absorb the mineral that the mountain shared with the stone while the rain masks the tears of the mountain and the stone

with time the stone couldnt stand and had to leave the mountain and was carried away to the sea because it was all eroded and reduced to bits of micro atoms but the flowers on the mountain were also eaten by rats who were eaten by hawks who were eaten by sharks who excreted the mountain into the sea

and in the sea the mountain and the stone fused again and formed another stone that eventually shone as bright as magma and the Sun.
